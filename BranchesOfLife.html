Branches Of Life

In this world, species are primarily classified into two main branches: "Natura" and "Divine". Additionally, there exists the category of "Artificial", which encompasses robots and other artificially created beings. There's also "The Logic", comprising the original creators of the universe, who occupy a distinct category of their own.
