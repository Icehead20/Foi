Artificial

Artificial is a distinct branch of life comprising creatures whose origins differ from those of Natura and Divine entities. These beings did not come into existence through natural processes or divine creation but were instead crafted by individuals from existing branches, often incorporating mechanical components or metallic materials. Classification within this branch is based on the method and location of their creation.
