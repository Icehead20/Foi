Borundo No Kami

Borundo No Kami are Civil Divine Wormterrein native of Ejpon and Terraido in Desautala, their original place of creation.

Description

Distinct from other hand-made Wormterrains of Desautala (such as Shokeimoji-Me and Shokeimoji-Paharo), Borundo No Kamis differ in their overall shape, colors, and capabilities.

They're crafted in molds where specific portions of Gravitry are strategically positioned at the core to grant them gravity manipulation abilities. Subsequently, a blend of clay and Wormterrain liquid fills the mold. Once solidified, the Wormterrain assimilates the structure, birthing a new Borundo No Kami.

Unlike the others, their gravity manipulation doesn't make them float. Instead, it enhances mobility in their ponytails, allowing a more natural movement that they can control almost like a limb.

Created by Queenork with assistance from the inhabitants of the old Terraido kingdom, Borundo No Kamis were initially employed in delivery services, as thinkers, and as repositories of memory.

The most esteemed among them served as guardians in the queen's chambers.

Once considered paragons of beauty among other Wormterrains, marrying one was seen as a significant status elevation.

While easily provoked, Borundo No Kamis are not inherently violent but can be brusque. A calm Borundo No Kami that trusts someone can be remarkably gentle and even affectionate.

Since the cessation of hand-made Wormterrain production in Terraido, they have become increasingly scarce. Most reside in the kingdoms of Desautala, with only a few scattered across other regions.

The decline of the Terraido Kingdom has led to hardships for the Borundo No Kami, resulting in a widespread sense of purposelessness among the population in recent years.
